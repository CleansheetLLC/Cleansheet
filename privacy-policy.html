Privacy Policy

Cleansheet LLC Educational Platform

Effective Date: 01 Oct 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

Cleansheet LLC ("Cleansheet," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Cleansheet platform, repository, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

Our Privacy Commitment:

  • Privacy-First Platform - Your privacy is our top priority
  • Ad-Free Experience - No advertising, no tracking pixels, no behavioral profiling
  • No Data Sharing - We categorically do not share user data with partners, advertisers, or third parties
  • No AI Training on User Data - Your content and interactions are never used to train our LLM models
  • Anonymized Analytics Only - We collect only anonymized usage data to optimize platform performance

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or use Platform features, we may collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Username or display name (for platform identification)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Profile information (optional: name, professional background, learning goals)
  • Account preferences (settings, notification preferences)

2.2 Educational Content and Progress

To provide educational services, we collect:

  • Learning progress and achievements (completed courses, skills assessments)
  • Project submissions and portfolio items (code, documents, project metadata)
  • Coach-learner interactions (session notes, milestone tracking)
  • Assessment results and skill evaluations
  • User-generated content (forum posts, comments, contributions)

2.3 Anonymized Usage Data

We collect anonymized and aggregated usage data to optimize the Platform:

  • Feature usage patterns (which features are used, how often)
  • Navigation flows (page views, time spent on features)
  • Performance metrics (load times, error rates)
  • Technical diagnostics (browser type, device type, screen resolution)
  • Platform interactions (clicks, searches, feature engagement)

Important: This data is anonymized before analysis and cannot be traced back to individual users.

2.4 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention only, not stored long-term)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device information (type, model, screen size)
  • Session data (login times, session duration)
  • Cookies and local storage (for session management only)

2.5 Communications

When you contact us, we collect:

  • Support request information (your questions, issues, feedback)
  • Email correspondence
  • Communication preferences

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 Primary Uses

We use your information only for the following purposes:

Account Management:

  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Authenticating your identity
  • Managing your profile and preferences
  • Providing password reset and account recovery

Educational Services:

  • Delivering personalized learning experiences
  • Tracking your progress and achievements
  • Matching learners with appropriate coaches
  • Facilitating coach-learner collaboration
  • Generating progress reports and skill assessments

Platform Optimization:

  • Improving platform features and functionality
  • Identifying and fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Optimizing performance and user experience
  • Developing new features based on anonymized usage patterns

Security and Fraud Prevention:

  • Protecting against unauthorized access
  • Preventing fraudulent activities
  • Monitoring for security threats
  • Maintaining platform integrity

Communication:

  • Sending essential service notifications
  • Providing support responses
  • Sharing important platform updates
  • Delivering requested educational content

3.2 What We DO NOT Do With Your Data

We categorically DO NOT:

  • Sell or rent your personal information to any third party
  • Share your data with partners or advertisers for marketing purposes
  • Use your content to train AI/LLM models or improve external AI systems
  • Display targeted advertising or behavioral tracking
  • Share your data with data brokers or analytics companies
  • Use your information for purposes outside of Platform services
  • Create user profiles for commercial purposes
  • Track you across other websites or applications

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

4.1 No Third-Party Sharing

Cleansheet categorically does not share your personal information with third parties, including:

  • Advertising networks or partners
  • Data brokers or analytics services
  • Marketing companies
  • AI model training companies
  • Social media platforms
  • Business partners or affiliates

4.2 Limited Exceptions

We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

Legal Compliance:

  • When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • To comply with valid government requests
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To protect our legal rights and property

Safety and Security:

  • To prevent harm to individuals or property
  • To investigate fraud or security incidents
  • To protect the safety of users or the public

Business Transfer:

  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (subject to equivalent privacy protections)

With Your Consent:

  • When you explicitly authorize data sharing for specific purposes

4.3 Service Providers

We may use carefully vetted service providers for:

  • Cloud hosting (Azure infrastructure for platform operation)
  • Email delivery (for essential service communications)
  • Payment processing (if applicable, PCI-compliant processors)
  • Security services (SSL/TLS certificates, DDoS protection)

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing services to Cleansheet.

5. AI AND MACHINE LEARNING

5.1 No User Data for Model Training

Your content and personal data are NEVER used to train our AI/LLM models.

Cleansheet's approach to AI:

  • Pre-trained models only - We use train our models using expert human-reviewed data
  • User controlled brokering - Cleansheet gives you complete control over external and auxiliary LLMs the Platform leverages to enable specific non-native capabilities
  • Zero user data training - Your educational content, projects, and interactions are not used for model improvement
  • Content privacy - Your submissions remain private and are not fed into AI training pipelines
  • Ethical AI use - AI features are designed to enhance your learning, not exploit your data

5.2 AI Features on Platform

AI features on the Platform (resume generation, content recommendations, skill assessments) operate using:

  • Your explicit inputs (only what you provide for specific tasks)
  • Pre-trained public models (not trained on Cleansheet user data)
  • Temporary processing (AI interactions are not stored for model training)
  • Opt-in functionality (you control when AI features are used)

6. DATA SECURITY

6.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL for all data transmission)
  • Encryption at rest (database encryption for stored data)
  • Password protection (bcrypt hashing for account passwords)
  • Access controls (role-based permissions, least privilege principle)
  • Security monitoring (intrusion detection, anomaly detection)
  • Regular security audits (vulnerability assessments, penetration testing)
  • Azure security features (leveraging Microsoft's enterprise security infrastructure)

6.2 Experimental Platform Notice

IMPORTANT: The Platform is in experimental development status.

While we implement reasonable security measures:

  • No absolute security guarantee can be made for experimental systems
  • Data breaches may occur despite our best efforts
  • Security measures are implemented on a best-effort basis
  • Users should not submit highly sensitive personal information (SSN, financial account numbers, health records)
  • We recommend strong, unique passwords and careful protection of account credentials

6.3 Your Security Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • Using strong, unique passwords
  • Notifying us immediately of suspected unauthorized access
  • Protecting your devices and network connections
  • Being cautious about information you choose to share publicly

7. DATA RETENTION

7.1 Active Accounts

While your account is active, we retain:

  • Account information - For the duration of your account
  • Educational progress - To track your learning journey
  • Projects and submissions - As part of your portfolio
  • Communication history - For support and service quality

7.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is deleted within 90 days
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics and platform improvement
  • Public contributions (if any) may remain anonymized
  • Backups may retain data for up to 180 days before complete deletion

7.3 Data Retention Periods

  • Account data - Duration of account + 90 days
  • Session logs - 30 days
  • Security logs - 1 year
  • Anonymized analytics - Indefinite (cannot be traced to individuals)
  • Support communications - 2 years
