## Risk Management

### Technical Risks
- **Complexity Documentation:** Some innovations may require simplification for clear technical disclosure
- **Trade Secret Balance:** Ensure sufficient detail for prior art without revealing proprietary algorithms
- **Integration Dependencies:** Document system interactions without exposing security vulnerabilities

### Mitigation Strategies
- Regular technical review with software architecture team
- Legal consultation for trade secret vs. disclosure balance
- Focus on system-level innovation rather than implementation-specific details

### Timeline Risks
- **Resource Availability:** Technical team availability may impact review schedules
- **Scope Creep:** Additional innovations may be identified during documentation
- **Quality vs. Speed:** Maintain comprehensive documentation standards despite timeline pressure

### Mitigation Approaches
- Prioritize highest-value innovations first
- Maintain flexible resource allocation across phases
- Establish clear quality gates and review processes
