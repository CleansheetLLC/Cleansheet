Abstract

The Distributed Content Delivery and Caching System revolutionizes content distribution through intelligent edge computing, predictive content placement, and adaptive caching strategies that collectively optimize content delivery performance while minimizing bandwidth costs and latency. This comprehensive system implements sophisticated content prediction algorithms, dynamic cache management, and intelligent request routing mechanisms that enable unprecedented content delivery efficiency at global scale. Key innovations include machine learning-driven content popularity prediction, geographically-aware cache placement optimization, real-time content invalidation, and adaptive compression techniques that provide superior performance compared to traditional CDN architectures while reducing operational costs.
