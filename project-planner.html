Project Planner

Plan and manage projects with RACI matrices, task scheduling, and work breakdown structures

The Project Planner helps you organize complex initiatives using industry-standard frameworks and visual tools. Build comprehensive RACI matrices to clarify roles and responsibilities across your team, create detailed work breakdown structures to decompose deliverables into manageable tasks, and develop Gantt chart timelines to track dependencies and milestones. Export your completed plans to JSON for documentation, share with stakeholders, or import existing project structures to iterate and refine your planning approach.

Your data is stored locally in your browser. All information is processed on your device and never sent to our servers.

We recommend exporting your project to JSON frequently using the Data menu to backup your work. Browser data can be lost if you clear your cache or use incognito/private mode.
